How to Play
Click a glowing cell to infect it. This ends your turn. Mutations spread via passive effects — they don't end your turn. Spend DNA pts on mutations any time between turns. Mutations cause symptoms — raising detection risk but boosting spread. The cure rolls back infection when complete. Hover mutations for details.
